Our Mission And Vision

Our Mission is to achieve the reputation of a quality, high standard and reliable goods and service provider in construction, farming and landscaping sectors of zambia, thereby contributing to the countries national development.

Our vision is to achieve 100% customer satisfaction by providing quality services and delivering quality produce at an affordable cost whille maintaining environmentally conscious practices. Out main objective as APACHI construction and Farming Limited is to be among the largest and professional general contruction, farming and landscaping companies in the country. We are always ready to take our achievements to the next level. We are growing and would always like to remain on the growing streak
